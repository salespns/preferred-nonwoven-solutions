Products We Offer

Carded Chemical Bond

Fibers bonded using chemical agents for versatile applications.

Thermal Bond

Heat-bonded fibers creating soft, yet strong materials.

Spunbond

Continuous filaments offering excellent strength and stability.

Needlepunch

Mechanically interlocked fibers for dense, durable fabrics.

Spunlace

Water-jet entangled fibers for soft, absorbent materials.

Ultrasonic Bond

Bonding via high-frequency vibrations for precise patterns.

Laminates

Multi-layered composite materials for enhanced properties.

Meltblown

Micro-fibers with superior filtration and barrier capabilities.

Knits

Interlocking looped yarns for flexible and stretchy fabrics.

Films

Thin, flexible sheets used as barriers or in composites.
