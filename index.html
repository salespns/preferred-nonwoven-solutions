Nonwoven Technologies We Offer

Spunbond

Continuous filaments are extruded, stretched, and laid into a web, offering excellent strength and stability.

Meltblown

Fine fibers are produced from molten polymer, creating a web with superior filtration and absorption properties.

SMS Composites

Multi-layer fabric (Spunbond-Meltblown-Spunbond) that combines strength with a high-efficiency bacterial barrier.

Needlepunch

Fibers are mechanically interlocked with barbed needles, resulting in dense, felt-like fabrics for durable applications.

Spunlace

Fibers are entangled using high-pressure water jets, creating soft, strong, and absorbent materials without binders.

Carded

Staple fibers are aligned to form a web, which can then be bonded. Ideal for creating lofty and soft products.

Airlaid

Cellulose fibers are formed into a web with air, producing a highly absorbent, paper-like fabric structure.

Wetlaid

Fibers are dispersed in water and collected on a screen, allowing for uniform webs with specialized fibers.
